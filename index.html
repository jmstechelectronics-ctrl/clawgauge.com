What it does

Built like a trading lab, not a hype machine.

The current focus is on disciplined automation support, strict pre-trade checks and a clear paper trail for every important decision.

Signal scanning

Continuously reviews market conditions to identify setups worth examining further.

Risk controls

Rules are checked before any trade path is considered acceptable for testing.

Testing workflows

Supports controlled checks, repeatable scenarios and careful validation before any expansion.

Decision logging

Records the reasons behind key actions so outcomes can be traced and reviewed later.

Human oversight

Automation is designed to support, not replace, judgement while the project remains in testing.

Why ClawGauge exists

A more disciplined approach to automation.

ClawGauge was built to explore a more disciplined approach to crypto trading automation - reducing emotional decision-making, improving trade visibility and enforcing stronger risk controls before capital is committed.

The site reflects the current state of the project: a serious technical build with a focus on process, evidence and restraint rather than performance claims.

Lower noise Filter out impulsive, low-quality actions.
Better visibility See why each decision was considered.
Stronger control Keep every test inside explicit guardrails.
